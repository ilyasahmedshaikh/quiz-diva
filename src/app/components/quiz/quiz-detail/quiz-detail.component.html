Can You See The Objects Hidden In The Ishihara Tests?

Did you know that it's much more likely for men to be colorblind than women? 1 in 200 women are born colorblind while 1 out of every 12 men are. The Ishihara color blind test was invented by Dr. Shinobu Ishihara in 1916. The test was originally used to help identify color blind soldiers in the military but became more widespread in 1917.

We have used the Ishihara test template and added our own images within the dots to test your color acuity. Within this quiz are questions that test if your eyes are affected by protanopia, deuteranopia, and tritanopia. If you issues answering most of these questions, it's quite possible you could be colorblind.
